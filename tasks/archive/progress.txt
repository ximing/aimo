## Codebase Patterns
- `view()` from `@rabjs/react` wraps components for reactive state; to use `forwardRef`, wrap with `forwardRef(...)` inside `view(forwardRef(...))` and close with `}))`
- `selectedRelations` state in MemoEditorForm uses `MemoListItemDto[]`; `memoId` is the unique identifier
- `textareaRef` is already declared in MemoEditorForm at line ~83; use it for `focusTextarea`
- MemoCard uses Tailwind `group-hover` classes for hover-visible action buttons
